Rowing in New Zealand is a well-respected and widely supported sport, known for producing world-class athletes and fostering strong values like discipline, resilience, and teamwork. It offers students and young athletes the chance to compete at local, national, and even international levels, often opening pathways to university scholarships and elite training programs. Despite early mornings and a demanding schedule, the rewards of personal growth and team spirit make rowing an unforgettable experience.
