What Does Rowing Cost?

Rowing is a rewarding but resource-intensive sport. Costs can vary greatly depending on the school or club, but families can expect to pay around $2,000 NZD per season. Let’s break it down:

Breakdown of Typical Costs

  • 🛶 Equipment rental: $500–$800
  • 👕 Uniforms: $100–$200
  • 🚍 Travel & accommodation: $400–$700
  • 🏆 Regatta entry fees: $150–$300
  • 👨‍🏫 Coaching fees: $300–$500
  • 💼 Miscellaneous (nutrition, physio, etc.): $100–$200

Financial Assistance

Some schools offer fundraising opportunities, payment plans, or sponsorships to help with costs. Talk to your coach or sports coordinator to see what support is available for your family.
